Greenberry is the clearest expression of Bernard's approach to web development for businesses: modern tools, fast performance, and a deliberate move away from bloated, high-maintenance website stacks.

## Goal

The goal is to give Irish businesses websites that are fast, professional, and easy to live with after launch. That means reducing unnecessary complexity while still delivering something polished and commercially effective.

## Purpose

Its purpose is to provide a dedicated brand and service model around Bernard's web work. Greenberry makes the offer easy to understand: clean builds, strong performance, simple hosting, and less long-term maintenance overhead than older CMS-driven setups.

## How It's Doing

Greenberry looks like one of the most mature and well-defined projects in the portfolio. It is active, commercially clear, and strong enough to have inspired adjacent niche projects like Churchify. Qualitatively, it seems to be doing very well as both a service business and a foundation for further ideas.
