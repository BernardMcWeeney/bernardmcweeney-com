Churchify is a niche web platform focused on churches and parishes, taking the general web development approach behind Greenberry and shaping it around a specific audience with distinct needs.

## Goal

The goal is to provide churches with websites that are clean, dependable, and straightforward to manage. Many organisations in this space need clarity and ease of use more than flashy features, so the aim is to give them something practical and well-suited to their day-to-day reality.

## Purpose

Its purpose is to turn an observed niche into a focused offering. Rather than serving every possible kind of client in the same way, Churchify creates a clearer message, a more tailored service, and a stronger fit for the organisations it is built to help.

## How It's Doing

Churchify looks like one of the more commercially grounded niche projects in the portfolio. It is active, its audience is well defined, and it benefits from growing out of work Bernard was already doing successfully. Qualitatively, it seems to be in a good position because the offer is specific and the need it addresses is easy to understand.
