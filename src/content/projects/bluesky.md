Bluesky is an ambitious platform idea built around consolidation. Instead of relying on a messy stack of disconnected tools, it imagines a single place where websites, apps, forms, products, events, and communication channels can work together.

## Goal

The goal is to reduce digital fragmentation. Bluesky aims to offer organisations one coherent platform rather than asking them to stitch together plugins, third-party services, and overlapping subscriptions.

## Purpose

Its purpose is to explore a more unified model for digital infrastructure. At a strategic level, that means taking the lessons from web development and consultancy work and pushing them toward something more productised, more integrated, and potentially more scalable.

## How It's Doing

Bluesky is still in development, and that fits the scale of the idea. It appears to be one of the broadest and most ambitious projects in the portfolio, so progress here is likely about refining the concept and defining the system clearly rather than declaring it finished too early. Right now it reads as promising and high-potential, but still in the stage where direction matters more than momentum.
